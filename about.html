About Hashbount

HashBount

HashBount is a leading private limited cybersecurity company that specializes in providing a wide range of cutting-edge security services to protect businesses and organizations from cyber threats. With a vast network and a team of highly skilled professionals, HashBount is at the forefront of safeguarding digital assets and ensuring the integrity of online operations.

Brief history of the company

HashBount was founded in 2023 by two cybersecurity professionals, Muhammad Faiz and Ghost. The company was created out of a desire to provide businesses and organizations with the best possible cybersecurity protection. HashBount has since grown to become a leading provider of cybersecurity services in the region.

Team members

The HashBount team is made up of highly skilled cybersecurity professionals with a wide range of experience. The team includes pentesters, SOC analysts, security engineers, and security architects. All of the team members are certified by industry-leading organizations such as CompTIA, EC-Council, and ISACA.

What we stand for

HashBount is committed to providing our clients with the highest level of security. We believe that everyone has the right to be safe online, and we are dedicated to making that happen. We stand for transparency, integrity, and innovation. We are always looking for new ways to protect our clients from cyber threats.
